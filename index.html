Era Morph

“Era Morph” explores the metamorphosis of hand-spun textiles across the timeline of human existence—bridging ancient craftsmanship with speculative future-wear.

Past: intricate drapes, earthy vegetable dyes, rhythmic loom geometries.
Present: modern tailored separates, urban layering, slow-fashion consciousness.
Future: modular silhouettes, architectural pleats, metallic-infused handloom, structured 3D forms.

The Eight-Winged War

Those Who Fell: Askael, Seraphaen, Malzith, Nyxiel.
Those Who Stood: Vaelith, Elyndra, Oris, Thadon.

ASKAHA — Handcrafted Jewelry

A sustainable, handcrafted jewelry concept inspired by traditional Indian Kantha embroidery—merging culture with minimalistic modernity through rich storytelling and unique craftsmanship.

Kantha is more than a stitch; it is storytelling made from repurposed textiles. This project explores embedding Kantha into gemstone jewelry to create “modern heirlooms” that honor heritage while defining a contemporary aesthetic.
